Ration Distribution System: Empowering Communities

The main motive of this project is to create a transparent and efficient system for distributing government-provided commodities to individuals through ration cards. By digitizing the process and implementing QR code technology, we aim to provide easy access to information about available commodities and facilitate online donations. Through this system, rural area residents can easily identify and avail donated commodities, promoting fairness and inclusivity in distribution.
